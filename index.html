Borus Mountain is

Borus is one of the most beautiful and popular tourist destinations in the Krasnoyarsk Krai. It has been considered sacred to the Khakass people since ancient times, and esotericists speak of the mountain range's energetic wealth. The range comprises four peaks: Kubinskikh Revolyutsionerov Peak, Bolshoi (Glavny) Borus, Maly Borus, and Koshurnikov Peak. All four peaks are connected by gentle slopes. The highest point of the range is Bolshoi Borus, reaching 2,318 meters above sea level.

Flora and fauna

The national park's fauna is extremely diverse. It is home to 254 species, including both typical Siberian taiga animals and some Central Asian species: wolves, foxes, bears, hares, deer, badgers, lynxes, and even snow leopards. Rare bird species such as the mountain finch and nightjar are also found here.

Climbing Borus

The round-trip route is approximately 20 km long Total elevation gain: approximately 2,000 m Travel time: approximately 12 hours The route is rated 1B in difficulty, meaning it doesn't require special equipment and is accessible to healthy, active adults, but it's not an easy walk.

Sayano-Shushenskaya hydroelectric power station

The Sayano-Shushensky Hydroelectric Complex is located on the Yenisei River. It includes the Sayano-Shushenskaya Hydroelectric Power Station, the downstream Mainsky counter-regulatory hydroelectric complex, and a coastal spillway.

Where is the Sayano-Shushenskaya Hydroelectric Power Station located?

The famous hydroelectric power station is located in Russia, in Siberia, in the Republic of Khakassia. The Sayano-Shushenskaya hydroelectric power station was built in Khakassia near the urban-type settlement of Cheryomushki, which is located 4 kilometers away—literally a 5-minute drive.

How to visit the Sayano-Shushenskaya Hydroelectric Power Station

For a long time, tourists were unable to visit the hydroelectric power station. Neither group nor individual tours were offered. However, in 2021, tours were launched—at first, only for power engineering students, but then the facility's management signed an agreement with tour operators.

About of Khakassia

Khakassia is a constituent entity of the Russian Federation, a republic within it. It is part of the Siberian Federal District and the East Siberian Economic Region. Its capital is Abakan.
